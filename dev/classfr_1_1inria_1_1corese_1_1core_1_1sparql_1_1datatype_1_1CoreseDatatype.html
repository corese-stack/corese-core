◆ compareTo()

int compareTo ( Object  d2)

Generic comparison between datatypes, used to sort the projection cache and to sort results.

It sorts deterministically different datatypes with their natural order (e.g. : numbers, strings, dates) The order is : Literal, URI, BlankNode string/literal/XMLliteral/boolean undef numbers are sorted by values string/literal/XMLLiteral/undef/boolean are sorted alphabetically then by datatypes (by their code) literals are sorted with their lang if any
