Checks whether a candidate edge matches a query edge.

May verify that edge labels match (Producer may have done the job properly). May verify edges such as rdf:type and check subsumption of type. Must verify that each candidate edge node match its query edge node, e.g. constant query nodes. (KGRAM does not perform the latter test and supposes that matcher does it) Matcher is not concerned by bindings that are checked by KGRAM.
