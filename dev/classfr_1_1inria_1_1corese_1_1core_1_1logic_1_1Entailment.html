Detailed Description

RDFS Entailment.

rdfs:domain rdfs:range rdfs:subPropertyOf rdfs:subClassOf owl:SymmetricProperty owl:inverseOf

subPropertyOf & subClassOf are not transitive in the graph but their instances are typed according to transitivity

Olivier Corby, Edelweiss INRIA 2010
