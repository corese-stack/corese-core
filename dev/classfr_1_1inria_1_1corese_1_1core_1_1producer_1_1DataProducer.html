Detailed Description

Transient Dataset over graph in order to iterate edges Use case: Producer getEdges() default or named graphs, from or from named Default graph: eliminate duplicate edges during iteration May take edge level into account for RuleEngine optimization Example: graph.getDefault().from(g1).iterate(foaf:knows) graph.getNamed().minus(list(g1 g2)).iterate(us:John, 0) graph.getDefault().iterate().filter(ExprType.GE, 100) – filter (?object >= 100)
