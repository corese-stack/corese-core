Detailed Description

Emulate method inheritance on datatype hierarchy return datatype list of rdf term datatype hierarchy is: dt:entity dt:resource dt:uri dt:bnode dt:literal dt:standard dt:extended.

xsd datatypes are under dt:standard dt datatypes are under dt:extended

xsd datatype hierarchy is not defined yet, it is flat under dt:standard

method is attached to datatype like this: @type dt:literal @type dt:standard @type xsd:int

method is available for datatypes equal or under the @type
